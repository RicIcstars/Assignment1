    About me

    In 2020, I embarked on my coding journey with enthusiasm, eager to explore the world of programming. However, as challenges arose, I faced the daunting task of navigating through them without a support system, leading to moments of discouragement. Frustrated by the high costs of bootcamps, my motivation took a hit. Determined to persevere, I sought alternative ways to enhance my skills, turning to tech podcasts and videos featuring seasoned programmers for inspiration. The turning point came when I delved into researching free programming courses, discovering valuable resources that reignited my passion for coding. Among these, i c stars emerged as a beacon, offering a community and learning environement that finally empowered me to resume and thrive on my tech journey.
